React Native Developer – Gaming Startup in Central Copenhagen


About Us

We're a small but ambitious gaming startup based in the heart of Copenhagen. Currently a tight-knit team of 3 (scaling to 5-6), we're building something exciting in the gaming space. Our studio combines creative freedom with technical excellence, and we're looking for someone who wants to be part of shaping a product from the ground up.


The Role

We're seeking a talented React Native Developer to join our core team. You'll have significant influence on both our technical architecture and product direction, working closely with the founders and the rest of the team to bring our game to life.


What you'll do:

  • Develop and maintain our mobile + web game using React Native

  • Architect scalable, performant solutions for complex gaming features

  • Help shape the technical roadmap

  • Write clean, maintainable code

  • Participate in code reviews and contribute to our engineering culture


Requirements

Must have:

  • Strong experience with React Native and modern JavaScript/TypeScript

  • Understanding of mobile app performance optimization

  • Experience with CI/CD

  • Experience shipping mobile applications to App Store and Google Play

  • Ability to work independently and take ownership

  • Fluent in English (Danish is a plus, but not required)

Nice to have:

  • Experience in gaming or real-time interactive applications

  • Knowledge of animation libraries (Reanimated, Lottie, etc.)

  • Experience working in early-stage startups

  • An AI optimised workflow


What we offer

Work environment:

  • Office in central Copenhagen (easy access via public transport)

  • Flexibility for remote work when needed

  • Modern development setup and tools

Culture:

  • Relatively flat hierarchy with rich possibilities of having impact on the product and company.

  • Collaborative, creative work environment

  • Opportunity to grow with the company

Location: On-site in Copenhagen city center, with flexible work-from-home options


How to apply

Send us your CV and a brief message about why you're interested in joining us. We'd love to hear about:

  • Projects you're proud of (links appreciated!)

  • What excites you in general

  • Why you're considering a startup environment


Start date: As soon as possible

Employment type: Full-time

Location: Copenhagen, Denmark


We review applications on a rolling basis and conduct interviews continuously. Don't hesitate to reach out if you have questions about the role!

For more information or questions please contact us at jsa@loofers.dk or phone number +4522761196

About Loofers Loofers ApS is a Danish gaming platform that creates excitement and joy through interactive competitions for exclusive prizes. Licensed by the Danish Gambling Authority (Spillemyndigheden, ref. J.nr. 24-466426), we offer a unique combination of entertainment and the opportunity to win attractive products – from gadgets and gift cards to fashion and innovative technology. What We Do At Loofers, users can reserve tickets for competitions to win exclusive products. To qualify their tickets, they must complete a simple game, where the top 95% advance to the prize draw. The concept is designed to be accessible to everyone – we even have a practice mode where users can train before participating. Our Values We're aware that our industry carries responsibility and scrutiny. While some larger operators prioritize growth at any cost, we've taken a different path. Loofers is built on a slow-pace gaming model – a format that research shows has no link to gambling addiction. We're fully licensed and compliant, but we go further: actively monitoring user behavior, implementing safeguards, and partnering with organizations like ROFUS and StopSpillet. We're the small player choosing to do things differently – prioritizing user welfare and sustainable entertainment over aggressive monetization. User experience: We continuously update our platform with new features and products to create the best possible experience Our Platform Loofers is available as an iOS app and has experienced growth since launch. We constantly work to improve the user experience through ongoing technological updates and new features.
